whoami

Hello, my name is Karan Grover.

I am a graduate student at the University of Wisconsin-Madison with a focus on building reliable and large-scale distributed systems. Before coming to UW-Madison, I worked as a Software Engineer at Microsoft Research for more than three years where I worked on a wide variety of projects like developing a cloud-native and secure ML Service using IntelSGX in collaboration with Azure Confidential Computing, building a blockchain protocol from scratch and developing a project management solution for one of India’s biggest Philanthropic Organization - Child Fund India. I learned how to build performant and scalable systems at MSR, and to strengthen my skills, I enrolled in the Master’s Program at UW-Madison. My courses focus on a mix of Systems and Machine Learning. Last Summer I interned at Meta(Facebook) as a part of their Inference Platform Team in the AI Infrastructure Group. My project was to develop an A/B Testing Framework for their new Inference Service. I was responsible for taking the project from design to production by working with different stakeholders to understand the engineering requirements and tradeoffs.
